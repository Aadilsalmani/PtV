Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2025
Last Updated: October 2025

1. About The P2V

The P2V — Places to Visit (accessible at https://thep2v.com) helps users discover, explore, and organize tourist destinations through an interactive map. We respect your privacy and collect minimal data necessary to make the app functional and enjoyable.

2. Information We Collect

The P2V does not collect personal information (such as your name, contact info, or photos). However, the app may collect limited technical and analytical data to ensure performance and improve user experience.

Data Type Description Purpose Shared With
Precise Location Your GPS location (latitude & longitude) when you allow access. To display nearby destinations and calculate travel distances. Not shared with third parties.
App Interactions Anonymous usage data (e.g., filters used, directions opened). Analytics and feature improvement. Google Analytics / Firebase.
Crash Logs & Diagnostics Automatic reports if the app encounters an error or crash. To identify issues and enhance app stability. Firebase Crashlytics.
Device Identifiers Non-personal identifiers generated by Firebase or your device. Analytics and performance optimization. Google Firebase / Analytics.

3. How We Protect Your Data

  • All communications are encrypted using HTTPS.
  • We never sell or trade user data.
  • No access to personal files, contacts, or media is requested.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We do not store user data on our own servers. Analytics and crash reports are processed by trusted Google services. You can clear local data anytime from your device settings.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Analytics — helps us understand app usage (Firebase Privacy).
  • Firebase Crashlytics — helps identify crashes (Crashlytics Privacy).

6. Permissions Used

  • Location (Precise) — shows your position and nearby places.
  • Internet Access — to load map data and analytics.

7. Children’s Privacy

The app is designed for a general audience and not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Future Updates or Ads

Currently, The P2V does not display advertisements. If ads are introduced in the future, they will comply with Google Play policies and this privacy policy will be updated accordingly.
