Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2025

Welcome to The P2V — Places to Visit (“The P2V”, “we”, “our”, “us”). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app or visit https://thep2v.com.

1. Information We Collect

  • Location Data: We access your location (with your permission) to show nearby destinations and calculate distances. Location data is not stored or shared with third parties.
  • Usage Data: We use Google Analytics and Firebase to understand app usage trends, feature performance, and improve user experience.
  • Feedback Data: If you submit feedback, we may store the message you send along with an approximate timestamp and optional location details for improvement purposes.

2. How We Use the Data

  • To display places near your location.
  • To improve performance, usability, and reliability of the app.
  • To fix bugs, measure engagement, and plan new features.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data. Data may be shared only with Google (Firebase & Analytics) for technical processing under their privacy terms.

4. Data Storage and Retention

All app preferences (filters, status selections, etc.) are stored locally on your device (LocalStorage). No personally identifiable data is stored on our servers.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

The P2V uses local storage to save your filters and visit preferences for convenience. We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking across websites.

6. Third-Party Services

The app relies on:

  • Google Firebase (for data and analytics)
  • Google Maps Directions (for external navigation links)
  • Leaflet.js (for map rendering)

Each third-party service follows its own privacy policy.

7. Your Choices

  • You can revoke location access at any time via your device settings.
  • You can clear app data to remove any locally stored preferences.

8. Children’s Privacy

The P2V is designed for general audiences and not targeted at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
