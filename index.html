About The P2V

Discover the World, Smarter.

The P2V (Places to Visit) is a next-generation travel mapping web app that helps explorers, tourists, and digital nomads discover the world’s most exciting destinations — from Tier I landmarks to hidden local gems — using real-time maps, smart filters, and personalized tracking.

  • 📍 Track your live location as you travel.
  • 🔍 Filter attractions by popularity or distance.
  • 🗂️ Save and manage visited places easily.
  • 🧭 Get instant directions via Google Maps.

Whether you’re planning your first trip or curating your travel bucket list, The P2V makes exploration effortless, data-driven, and fun.

🌍 Built with passion by Aadil Salmani — blending geospatial intelligence, design clarity, and data-driven exploration.
